Leh Ladakh Package

Trip Length : 06 Nights & 7 Days

OVERVIEW

Leh is one of the most popular tourist destinations of India. It is extremely important that you acclimatize yourself to the high pressure of the high altitude so that you can enjoy your vacations here.

Daywise itinerary:

Day 1:

Catch a flight from Delhi airport to Leh.

Day 2:

Begin driving westwards of Leh to visit Alchi Monastery.

Day 3:

Start your day early and get transferred to Nubra Valley.

Day 4:

Day 4 is for visiting Samstanling Monastery-known for its serene ambiance and assembly halls with artifacts.

Day 5:

The day is scheduled for an excursion to the Pangong Lake.

Day 6:

The tour a little early to capture some early morning views around the lake.

Day 7:

In the morning of the last, get an assisted transfer to the Kushok Bakula airport to board flight for Delhi.
